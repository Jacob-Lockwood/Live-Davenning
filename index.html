Contact

Contact: jeremiahdaniellockwood@yahoo.com

We are actively seeking to expand our archive of live davenning bootleg recordings. Please reach out if you are in possession of recordings of classic era cantors in their element as prayer leaders on Shabbos or yontif. We would be grateful to add your personal recordings to the public archive. Through these privately made recordings we hope to develop greater knowledge of what creative cantorial davenning sounded like and to give future generations of singers access to hidden treasures of Jewish sacred music.
